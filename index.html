What to include in the Digital Time Capsule?

“In your digital time capsule, include personal messages to your future self, cherished photos, videos, and meaningful memories. Capture your goals, dreams, favorite music, and everyday moments. Document your thoughts on current events and trends, along with mementos of important relationships. This capsule will be a snapshot of who you are today, preserved for future reflection and discovery.”.
